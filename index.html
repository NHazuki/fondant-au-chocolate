*Fondant su Chocolate*

Custard Pudding is a very soft and delicate pudding made out of cream, milk, egg yolk and sugar. It is easy to make and an amazing dessert to go with your tea when friends and families are over.

    Ingredients(4 Servings)

Cupcake cups
- 50g of Dark Chocolate
Fondant au Chocolate
- 50g of Butter
- 1 Egg
- 15g of Corn Starch
- Ram (optional)
Topping
- Gently wipped Cream
    1. Making the Madeleine
      1. Add Egg yolk and Sugar into a bowl and mix
      2. Add the rest of the ingrediance for the Madelenine (except butter) to and mix well
      3. Add melted butter to step 2 and mix

      4. Pour step 3 untill it filles up 7/10 of the mold
      5. Place the batch into the oven for 13min at 180 degrees
        *If the surface turn burnt, cover it with aluminum foil

    2. Making the Icing
      1. Add Icing Sugar and Lemon Juice into a bowl and mix well
      2. Using a Cornet or a Spoon, decorate the Madeleine and enjoy your Lemon Madeleine!
